Responsible Social Media Use (Student Checklist)
Do’s
* Think before posting anything online
* Protect your personal information (phone number, address, passwords)
* Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
* Respect others’ opinions and communicate politely
* Verify information before sharing it
* Maintain a positive and professional online presence
Don’ts
* Don’t share sensitive personal or private information
* Don’t post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content
* Don’t spread rumors or fake news
* Don’t engage in cyberbullying or online arguments
* Don’t overshare your daily activities or location
* Don’t click on suspicious links or messages
